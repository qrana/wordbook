Welcome to wordbook!

Available word types:

  • n = Noun
  • v = Verb
  • a = Adjective
  • s = Sentence
  • e = Expression
  • d = Adverb
  • o = Other

Other is the default type for words. At the end of the line is the difficulty between 0 and 10, 10 being the default. The difficulty algorithm is still in development but currently decreases with correct answers and increases with incorrect. Future testing and printing options are coming based on word type and difficulty.
